Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

SabiStream (“the app”) keeps your data on your device and in your control. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

1 The app provides no content

SabiStream is a neutral media player. It contains no channels, streams, or playlists. You connect it to a service you already have (an Xtream Codes login, an M3U/M3U8 playlist, or a Stalker/Ministra portal). You are responsible for having the right to access the content you add.

2 Your playlist logins

When you add a playlist, your details (server address, username, password, portal/MAC, or playlist URL) are stored encrypted on your device using Android’s encrypted storage. They are sent only to the IPTV service you entered, in order to log in and stream. They are never sent to us or to any third party.

3 What stays on your device

Your favourites, watch history, “continue watching” positions, reminders, category customisations, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or receive them. Logging out, using the in-app reset, or uninstalling deletes this data.

4 Backups

A backup you create is an encrypted file saved wherever you choose (your device, Google Drive, email, a USB drive). We do not receive it, and only this app can open it.

5 Error and crash reports

The app never sends data silently. If you choose to send feedback or an error report (for example, after a crash), it prepares a report and opens your email app so you can send it to the developer. Technical reports are encrypted so that only the developer can read them, and login passwords are removed before any diagnostics are included. A report may contain the app version, your device model and Android version, and technical error details. Sending is always your choice.

6 No analytics, tracking, or ads

The app contains no third-party analytics, no advertising, and no tracking software. We do not build a profile of you, and we do not sell data.

7 Network connections

The app connects over the internet only to: the IPTV service(s) you add (to log in, load your channel/movie/series lists and programme guide, and stream); any programme-guide (XMLTV) sources you add; and artwork addresses provided by your service. It also connects to your email provider when you choose to send feedback. It makes no other connections.

8 Permissions

  • Internet & network state — to connect to your service and stream.
  • Notifications — to show programme reminders and the recording notification.
  • Foreground service & wake lock — to keep a scheduled recording running.
  • Exact alarm — to start scheduled recordings on time (granted by you when needed).
  • Run at startup — to re-arm scheduled recordings after a restart.

9 Children

SabiStream is a general-purpose media player, is not directed at children, and does not knowingly collect information from children.

10 Your control and deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you can delete it at any time by logging out, using the in-app reset, or uninstalling. Backup files you created stay wherever you saved them until you delete them.

11 Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes; the “last updated” date reflects the latest version.
